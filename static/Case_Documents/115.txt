Document_Number :115 Case ID 96185770 The remains in this case were discovered buried in the yard of a businessman residing in a small Indian community in central Region. Prior to this discovery, the homeowner, who had a known record of spousal abuse,had been a suspect in the disappearance of his wifein January of 2020. However, due to a lack of concrete evidence, the case was closed.  On the 5thof February, 2020, a neighbor to the homeowner reported that his dog had brought a bone into his house. Upon this discoverythe case was reopened and search warrant was issued to the home of the missing woman and her husband.  Through excavation, the partial skeleton in question was recovered. No personal items were found with the set of remains
